How to Use This Admin Panel

Adding Product Images

You have 3 options to add product images:

  • Option 1: Upload from your computer (click the upload box)
  • Option 2: Use free image hosting:
    • Go to ImgBB.com
    • Upload your product photo
    • Copy the "Direct Link" URL
    • Paste it in the image URL field
  • Option 3: Use Google Drive:
    • Upload photo to Google Drive
    • Right-click → Get shareable link
    • Change "view" to "download" in the URL
    • Paste the link

Setting Prices

  • Check Jiji for current market prices
  • Price competitively but not too cheap
  • Consider student discounts for KNUST
  • Update prices regularly based on demand

Mobile-Friendly Tips

  • Use your phone to take product photos
  • Ensure good lighting for clear images
  • Use simple backgrounds (white or dark)
  • Crop photos to focus on the product

Keeping Products Updated

  • Add new products as you get them
  • Update prices when they change
  • Mark sold-out products as unavailable
  • Add seasonal or special offers

This admin panel saves data locally in your browser. Clear browser data will reset products. For permanent storage, consider Firebase or a backend.
